J-Calc Scientific Calculator and Unit Coverter
J-Calc is a feature rich calculator and unit converter that is designed to be very easy to use.

It includes:
 - Math (square root, Logarithm, etc.)
 - Trig (Sin, Cos, ArcSin, Deg, Rad, Grad, etc.)
 - Logic (Hex, Oct, Bin, And, Or, etc.)
 - Weight conversions
 - Distance Conversions
 - Area Conversions
 - Volume Conversions
 - Constants (Pi, e, and Euler-Mascheroni, more to come)
 - Memory (can store hundreds of memories)
